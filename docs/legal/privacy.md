# Privacy Policy

**Effective date: April 24, 2026**

> **Draft notice:** This document is a draft placeholder and will be reviewed by legal counsel before the official launch.

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how astix ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our platform and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable French data protection law.

## 2. Data Controller

The data controller for personal data processed through astix is:

**astix**
France
[privacy@astix.io](mailto:privacy@astix.io)

## 3. Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

- **Account information:** name, email address, and credentials provided at registration.
- **Usage metrics:** feature usage, session duration, tool invocation counts, and error reports — used to improve the platform.
- **Code metadata:** symbol names, file paths, and structural relationships (e.g., call graphs) derived from your codebase during indexing. This is structural metadata, not source code content.

**Self-hosted deployments:** For users running astix on their own infrastructure, code indexing happens entirely on your machines. astix never receives, processes, or stores your source code.

## 4. Your Own Keys and Models (BYOK/BYOM)

When you configure astix with your own LLM or embedding API keys (Bring Your Own Key / Bring Your Own Model), your code is sent directly from your infrastructure to your chosen LLM provider according to that provider's terms. astix never processes or stores your source code in hosted mode beyond what is strictly necessary for indexing structural metadata.

## 5. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

- Provide, operate, and improve the astix platform.
- Authenticate users and manage account access.
- Respond to support requests and communicate service updates.
- Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties and do not use it for behavioral advertising.

## 6. Data Storage and Security

All personal data for hosted services is stored in the European Union. We apply industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, and regular security reviews. Despite our efforts, no system is completely secure; we encourage you to use strong passwords and report any suspected security issues to [privacy@astix.io](mailto:privacy@astix.io).

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right of access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right of rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Right of erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
- **Right to data portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Right to object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [privacy@astix.io](mailto:privacy@astix.io). We will respond within 30 days.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, personal data is purged within 30 days, except where retention is required by applicable law.

## 9. Cookies

astix uses only functional cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies, third-party advertising cookies, or analytics cookies that profile your behavior. No cookie consent banner is required beyond this disclosure.

## 10. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for infrastructure and operations (e.g., cloud hosting providers). These providers are bound by data processing agreements and may not use your data for their own purposes. We will update this section with a full list of sub-processors before launch.

## 11. Children's Privacy

astix is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page and, for material changes, notify registered users by email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
